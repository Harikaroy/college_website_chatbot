                   GPP-Government Polytechnic which is located in Proddatur is affiliated with State Board of Technical Education and Training. The college has established for the students to nurture them professional skills with adequate knowledge.The experienced faculty of the college motivates the students to achieve their career goals.

                                    The Govt-Polytechnic,Proddatur was started in the year 1959 with three main Engineering branches namely civil,Mechanical,Electrical Engg with an intial intake of 40 in each branch.Later in the 1985 4th branch namely Electronics & Communication Engg with an intake of 50 and in the year 2006 a fifth branch namely diploma in computers with an intake of 40 are added.Our polytechnic is one of the ten polytechnics selected for starting 2nd section in the institutes with 4 branches namely IT,ECE,EEE,Sandwich branches from the 2008-2009.

        ELECTRONICS AND COMMUNICATION ENGINEERING(ECE):

                Diploma in Electronics and Communication Engineering(ECE) is a 3-year course that focuses on understanding electronics networks and devices, electric magnetic fields, computer fundamentals, telecommunications, and control systems. ECE diploma courses can be done from any institution after completing class 10th. Diploma in ECE fees is usually divided semester-wise and can differ in different colleges. Diploma in Electronics and Communication Engineering admission can be through both merit or entrance based, depending upon the institution. The candidates must fulfill the minimum eligibility criteria for Diploma in Electronics and Communication Engineering before applying to any institution. An ECE diploma holder has diverse job opportunities in front of them in public as well as private organizations along with an average salary of about 3-4 lakh per annum in starting.
